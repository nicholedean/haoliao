OUR STORY

Restaurants waste a massive amount of food every day. In Malsysia alone, researches estimate retaurants waste up to 15,000 tonnes of food daily, with 3,000 tonnes still fit to consume. This amount is enough to feed two million hungry people.

Our application, Fed Up, aims to change this. We want to provide a way to sell this food back to the public, on the cheap helping to reduce food waste.

Nearly 40% of food is wasted globally... most of it ending up in landfills.
